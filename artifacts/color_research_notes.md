# Kid-friendly palette quick references

## Quick references used
- Duolingo brand ecosystem (recognizable cheerful learning vibe):
  - Core green family around **#58CC02** (friendly growth/success cue)
  - Sky blue support around **#1CB0F6** (trust + playfulness)
  - Warm yellow accent around **#FFC800** (reward/progress highlight)
- Common kid learning UI conventions:
  - Bright but soft backgrounds (mint/sky tint) to reduce visual fatigue
  - High-contrast dark text on vivid surfaces for readability
  - Distinct semantic colors for success/warning/danger

## Derived token direction
- Primary CTA: green (`#58CC02`) with dark text for contrast
- Secondary highlight: sky blue (`#1CB0F6`)
- Accent/progress reward: yellow (`#FFC800`)
- Soft surfaces: mint/sky tint (`#EAF8DC`, `#E3F5FF`)
- Text: deep neutral (`#1F2937`), muted body (`#5B6476`)
- Border: cool soft neutral (`#D7E6EF`)

## Contrast policy
- Prefer dark text (`#1F2937`) on bright green/yellow buttons and chips.
- Keep white text only on deeper tones (e.g., `primaryDeep`).
- Maintain clear border separation on pale cards.
